Worth a call

Signs it's time to get in touch

Spotted one of these? It's worth a quick call. Early attention usually means a simpler, cheaper fix.

  • No heating or no hot water
  • Radiators cold at the top or slow to warm
  • Banging, gurgling or whistling noises
  • Boiler pressure keeps dropping
  • Error codes or the pilot light going out
  • Boiler over ~10 years old or expensive to run
