Ventilation Units & Filters

Service, repair and installation of ventilation systems including MVHR, plus scheduled filter replacements.

Good ventilation keeps air fresh, controls condensation and protects your building. We service, repair and install ventilation units, and keep filters fresh.

Mechanical ventilation (including MVHR heat-recovery systems) is increasingly common in modern flats and commercial spaces. Like any system, it works best with regular maintenance.

We service and repair ventilation units, install new systems, and run scheduled filter-replacement programmes so air quality and efficiency stay where they should be.

Common questions

How often should ventilation filters be changed?

It depends on the system and environment, but most benefit from filter changes every few months. We can set up a schedule so it's never forgotten.

What is MVHR?

Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery supplies fresh air while recovering heat from outgoing air, efficient and common in modern, well-insulated buildings.
