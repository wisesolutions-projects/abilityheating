Supply, installation, servicing and repair of unvented and vented hot-water cylinders for powerful, reliable hot water.

Want strong, mains-pressure hot water to every tap and shower? A correctly installed unvented cylinder delivers it, safely certified and built to last.

Unvented cylinders give excellent hot-water pressure without a loft tank. They must be installed and serviced by a qualified engineer for safety, which is exactly what we do.

We supply and fit new cylinders, service existing ones (including the safety components), and repair faults such as leaks, poor recovery and discharging valves.

Common questions

Why does my unvented cylinder need annual servicing?

Unvented cylinders contain safety components (like the expansion vessel and pressure-relief valves) that must be checked yearly to keep the system safe and within warranty.

Water is dripping from an outside pipe. Is that normal?

Occasional discharge can be normal, but continuous dripping usually points to a fault. We'll diagnose and fix it before it wastes water or causes damage.
