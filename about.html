Our story

Built on doing the job properly

Ability Heating & Gas was founded on a simple idea: heating work should be safe, honest and done to a standard you'd be happy to put your name to.

Led by gas engineer Abhishek Yadav, we've grown by word of mouth across South-East London: homeowners, landlords and managing agents who want a contractor that communicates clearly, prices fairly and gets it right first time.

From a single boiler service to commercial plant rooms and whole-block HIU maintenance, we bring the same care to every job, large or small.

Accreditation

Gas Safe registered: 535881

Every gas engineer working legally in the UK must be on the Gas Safe Register. Ours is registration number 535881. It means our gas work is carried out by competent, qualified engineers and certified to current regulations.
