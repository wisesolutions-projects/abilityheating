Heat Interface Units (HIU)

HIU installation, servicing, fault diagnosis and repair for flats and developments on communal and district heating networks.

Get a free quote 07988 886674

Heat interface units are a core specialism. We understand communal heating inside-out, so residents get reliable heat and hot water, and managing agents get a contractor who knows the systems.

HIUs deliver heating and hot water to individual flats from a communal or district heating network. When they're set up and maintained well, they're efficient and quiet; when they're not, you get lukewarm water, noise and call-outs.

We install, service and repair all common HIU makes, optimise performance, and work with managing agents on planned maintenance across whole blocks.

What we cover

  • HIU installation for new & existing flats
  • Annual HIU servicing & optimisation
  • Plate heat exchanger descaling
  • Fault diagnosis & repair
  • Communal / district heating expertise
  • Block-wide planned maintenance
Gas Safe registered (535881). All gas work is carried out and certified to current regulations by qualified engineers.

Common questions

Do you work with managing agents on whole blocks?

Absolutely. We handle block-wide HIU servicing and planned maintenance, with clear reporting for managing agents and freeholders.

My hot water is slow or lukewarm. Can you help?

Often yes. Poor HIU performance is frequently down to scaling, settings or a worn component, all things we diagnose and put right.
